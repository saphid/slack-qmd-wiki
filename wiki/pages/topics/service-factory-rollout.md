# Service Factory Rollout

Service factory work is tracked as a DevOps/platform rollout that required end-to-end proof, review availability, and bug-squashing through later code paths before it could be considered done.

Current synthesis from the first wiki pass:

- The work had progressed through multiple PRs and was close to completion by late April 2026.
- Remaining issues were appearing in later code paths that could only be fully tested after earlier pieces were in place.
- Leadership pressure was to focus energy on finishing service factory ahead of lower-priority side work.
- The promotion pipeline was expected to be shorter than the initial service path, but still needed its own end-to-end testing.
